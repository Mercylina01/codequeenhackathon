About AlumniArc

AlumniArc is a digital platform designed to connect current and alumni Code Queens through mentorship, networking, collaboration and leadership. The platform promotes digital sisterhood by empowering young women in technology to share opportunities, inspire innovation and build stronger communities together.

About Code Queen

What is Code Queen?

Code Queen is a program in Uganda created by Educating the Children (ETC) in 2019 that trains young women in coding, software development, digital skills and career readiness. It mainly targets unemployed or underserved young women who may not have had access to tech education before — and has grown into one of the leading women-in-tech programs in Uganda.
